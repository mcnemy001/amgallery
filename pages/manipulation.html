Manipulation

Manipulation art is a type of digital art that involves using image editing software to alter or manipulate existing photographs or graphics to create a new, unique image. This can involve changing the colors, adding or removing elements, distorting perspectives, and more. Manipulation artists use their technical skills and creativity to produce surreal, dreamlike, or otherworldly images that can evoke emotion and tell a story.
