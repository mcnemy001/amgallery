Vector/Vexel

Vexel is a term used to describe a specific style of digital art that combines elements of vector art and pixel art. In vexel art, images are created using vector shapes and paths, but the fills and shading are created using pixel-based techniques, giving the image a hybrid look that combines the clean lines and scalability of vector art with the organic feel and texture of pixel art. Vexel art is commonly used in graphic design, illustrations, and digital art.
