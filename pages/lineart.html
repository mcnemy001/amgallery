Line Art

Line art is a type of illustration that consists of distinct, continuous lines that are used to define an image. Line art is typically drawn using a pen, pencil, or other similar tool, and it is often characterized by its lack of shading or color. Instead, the lines themselves are used to create the form and texture of the image. Line art can be used to create simple sketches, detailed drawings, or anything in between, and it is a fundamental component of many forms of visual art, including comics, cartoons, and technical illustrations.
