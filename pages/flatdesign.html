Flat Design

Flat design is a style of graphic design that emphasizes minimalism and simplicity, characterized by two-dimensional illustrations and flat colors. Flat design is a departure from more traditional, three-dimensional designs that incorporate drop shadows, textures, and gradients, and it typically uses basic geometric shapes, bold lines, and solid colors. Flat design is often used to create clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces for websites, mobile applications, and other digital products, and it prioritizes functionality over ornamentation.
